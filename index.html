Industrial Placements

General Information

A lot of students will choose to do an industrial placement to enhance their technical and transferrable skills. This provides them with a head start to securing a job after their degree as they will enter the work force with a years' worth of invaluable experience. A lot of major companies offers this as an option as they look to invest into talent early. This website will help you ace applications, use the buttons to the right to browse the different sections.
